Questions on Civic Education

Civic Education Questions and Answers

Question 1: What is the importance of voting in a democratic society?

Answer: Voting is a fundamental right and responsibility in a democratic society, allowing citizens to participate in the decision-making process and shape the future of their country.

Question 2: Explain the concept of the rule of law.

Answer: The rule of law ensures that all individuals and institutions, including the government, are subject to and accountable to the law. It promotes fairness, justice, and equal treatment for all.

Question 3: What are the three branches of the government, and what are their main functions?

Answer: The three branches are Executive (enforces laws), Legislative (makes laws), and Judicial (interprets laws). They provide a system of checks and balances to prevent the abuse of power.
