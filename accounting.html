Questions on Accounting

Accounting Questions and Answers

Question 1: What is the accounting equation?

Answer: The accounting equation is Assets = Liabilities + Equity, representing the fundamental relationship between a company's assets, liabilities, and equity.

Question 2: Define the term "depreciation."

Answer: Depreciation is the systematic allocation of the cost of a long-term asset over its useful life, reflecting the wear and tear or obsolescence of the asset.

Question 3: What is the purpose of a trial balance?

Answer: A trial balance is prepared to ensure that the total debits equal total credits in the accounting records, helping to identify errors before financial statements are prepared.
