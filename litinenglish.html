Questions on Literature-In-English

Literature Questions and Answers

Question 1: Who wrote the play "Romeo and Juliet"?

Answer: William Shakespeare

Question 2: In which novel is the character Atticus Finch featured?

Answer: "To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee

Question 3: What is the central theme of George Orwell's "1984"?

Answer: Dystopian society, government surveillance, and the impact of totalitarianism on individual freedom.
